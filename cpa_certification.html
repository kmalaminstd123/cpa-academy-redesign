How to become CPA: There are two routes to become CPA

Route 1: Qualification Assessment

Eligibility:

  • Any recognized professional qualification holder can become CPA through qualification assessment route.
Procedure:
  • Step-1: Submission of Application along with CV and all relevant qualification documents.
  • Step-2: Once accepted, make the payment.
  • Step-3: Certified Senior Professional in Project Management
  • Step-4: Result publication & Certificate issuance.
Route 2: Competency Assessment

Eligibility:

  • Graduation/Masters in Finance/Accounts + 3 years’ experience in Finance/Accounts.
  • Graduation/Masters in Commerce or business + MBA in Finance/Accounts/Banking + 10 years’ experience.
Procedure:
  • Step-1: Submit Application with CV and qualifications.
  • Step-2: Make payment once accepted.
  • Step-3: Self-prepare using provided guidelines.
  • Step-4: Attend the assessment.
  • Step-5: Receive result and CPA credentials.
