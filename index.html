Why Study Accounting

Accounting is a critical field that plays a vital role in the success of businesses and organizations. For those who are still deciding whether or not accounting is the right major for them, consider these benefits

Strong job outlook

Good news for those interested in accounting or auditing! The BLS predicts a 6% increase in demand for accountants and audits between 2019 and 2029, significantly higher than the average for all professions.

Transferable skills

Accounting provides a strong foundation in analytical and problem-solving skills that can be applied to various roles and industries.

High demand

Accounting professionals are in high demand, particularly those with specialized expertise in tax, audit, and financial reporting.

Career growth opportunities

Accounting offers a clear career path with opportunities for advancement and specialization. With the right skills and experience, you can progress to roles like controller, CFO, or partner at an accounting firm.
